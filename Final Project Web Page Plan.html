Plan

To complete the project, we will use the divide and conquer method. Everyone will take a page. For your page, you will come up with the content, the layout, and the look.

We must first have a meeting and agree on how it should look. Since every page will look similar, we can use a shared stylesheet. One person will be in charge of the site stylesheet to avoid conflicts. However you are free to add whatever you'd like to it, as well as maintain your own for your particular page.
