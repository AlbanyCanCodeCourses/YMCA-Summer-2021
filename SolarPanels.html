Specific Detail

Multiple cells make up a solar panel, and multiple panels (modules) can be wired together to form a solar array.

Solar panels are made of photovoltaic cells that capture sunlight and turn it into direct current electricity (DC electricity). This happens whenever sun rays are hitting your solar panels, rain or shine. The inverter converts the DC electricity into alternating current electricity, or AC electricity, to be used by your home. The electricity can now provide power to your home.
